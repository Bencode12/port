Overview

A comprehensive terminal-based chemistry computation toolkit designed for stoichiometric calculations, thermodynamic property estimation, equilibrium constant computations, and molecular mass calculations.

Features

  • Chemical equation balancing with automatic coefficient determination
  • pH and pOH calculations for acids, bases, and buffer solutions
  • Concentration conversions (molarity, molality, normality, ppm)
  • Reaction yield predictions and limiting reagent identification
  • Thermodynamic calculations (enthalpy, entropy, Gibbs free energy)
  • Equilibrium constant computations (Kc, Kp, Ka, Kb, Ksp)
  • Molecular mass and empirical formula calculations
  • Gas law computations (ideal and real gas equations)

Technical Implementation

Built with Python using numerical computation libraries for precise chemical calculations. The tool implements algorithms for reaction balancing, stoichiometric analysis, and thermodynamic property estimation. Designed with a focus on algorithmic precision and extensibility for advanced chemistry applications.

Use Cases

  • Academic chemistry coursework and problem sets
  • Laboratory preparation and yield calculations
  • Chemical reaction analysis and optimization
  • Independent study and research verification
